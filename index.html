Prep

10 mins

Total Time

10 mins

8 Servings:

Ingredients

  • 8 croutons

  • 8 (1-inch) wedges romaine lettuce

  • 8 (1 ounce) cubes cooked chicken

  • 8 (1/2-inch) cubes Parmesan cheese, or to
    taste

  • 8 bamboo toothpicks

  • 8 teaspoons Caesar salad dressing

Directions

Step 1

Thread a crouton, romaine lettuce wedge, chicken cube, and Parmesan cheese
cube onto each toothpick, respectively. Arrange toothpicks on a serving platter
and drizzle Caesar salad dressing over each.
